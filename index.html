Who We Are

Built by a Zambian,
Built for Zambia.

SyntaxSavvy ZM–SSZ was founded by a Computer Science student, Mizeezo Mukonka from the University of Zambia, who saw a gap — local businesses struggling without the right digital tools, and communities underserved by technology.

Instead of waiting for change, he decided to build it. He is alone and small but serious. He does not pretend to be something he is not. What he bring is real skill, genuine commitment, and a deep understanding of the Zambian market.

He is not just a service provider. He is building towards a more connected, digitally empowered Zambia — one solution at a time.

Our Mission

To solve real-world problems using innovative digital solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities across Zambia.

Our Vision

To become a leading tech solutions company in Zambia and beyond — known for building technology that truly matters.

Our Values

Honesty over hype. Impact over profit. Real solutions over flashy promises. We build small but think big.
